Our Services

List of services

Comprehensive Eye Exams:

Our comprehensive eye exams are designed to assess and maintain the health of your eyes. Our skilled optometrists will conduct various tests and assessments, including:

  1. Vision tests to determine your visual acuity and prescription needs. Refraction tests to determine the appropriate lens power for glasses or contact lenses.
  2. Eye health assessments to check for signs of diseases or conditions such as glaucoma, cataracts, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy. Regular comprehensive eye exams are essential for early detection and prevention of eye-related issues.
Diagnosis and Treatment

At Jamii Eye Care, our experienced eye care specialists are proficient in diagnosing and treating a range of common eye conditions, including:

  1. Cataracts: We offer advanced surgical procedures to remove cataracts and restore clear vision.
  2. Glaucoma: We offer comprehensive vision management and treatment options.
  3. Refractive Errors: Whether you have nearsightedness, farsightedness, or astigmatism, we offer individualized solutions including glasses, contact lenses, or refractive surgery.
Glasses and Contact Lens Fitting:

Our expert optometrists specialize in fitting and prescribing contact lenses for patients seeking an alternative to glasses. Our contact lens fitting process includes:

  1. Comprehensive eye examination to evaluate your eye health and determine if contact lenses are a suitable option for you.
  2. Measurement and assessment of your eyes to find the right contact lens size and type that provides optimal comfort and visual clarity.
  3. Detailed instructions on how to insert, remove, and care for your contact lenses to ensure proper hygiene and long-term eye health.
